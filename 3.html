About Amal Jyothi College of Engineering

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) is a premier institution in Kerala, known for its excellence in technical education and innovation. Established in 2001, AJCE is the first engineering college in Kerala to receive the prestigious NBA accreditation in its very first accreditation cycle.

  • B.Tech in Information Technology
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Automobile Engineering
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
